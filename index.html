About

I am a GIS Analyst and MGIS graduate student who loves solving real-world problems with spatial data. I’m especially drawn to projects that blend environmental insight, spatial data science, and clear, intuitive cartography. Across all my work, I’m driven by clear communication, thoughtful cartography, and building tools that help people understand and make decisions about the landscapes around them.

I currently work as a GIS Analyst at Apex Engineering Group supporting municipal infrastructure projects. My work includes water and sewer mapping, Survey123 design, Experience Builder tools, and asset management workflows that help cities use spatial data more effectively.

Sweet Sorghum Suitability Web App

Research • 2025

This project identifies high-potential regions for sweet sorghum production using Daymet climate data, soil characteristics, and land cover. I built a spatial suitability model, transformed the outputs into interactive layers, and designed a web app that allows users to explore climate-driven suitability patterns across Minnesota and the Midwest.

North Country Trail Planning Map

Client Project • 2025

Developed an interactive planning map for the North Country Trail Association to evaluate trail alignment options, land ownership, easement needs, and environmental constraints. This tool supports route refinement, internal coordination, and stakeholder discussions by consolidating authoritative data into a clear, easy-to-use web interface.
